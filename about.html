Our Principles

  1. Demonstrate the love of Jesus Christ We undertake our ministry to demonstrate the love and teaching of Jesus Christ.
  2. Promote Dignity and Hope We believe that every has something to contributeand something to gain fromcreating communities in which all people are brought out of poverty.
  3. Focus of community development We create opportunities training for all communities with the objective of establishing income producing ventures.
  4. Support Sustainable and Trensformational Development We view our ministry as successful when it transforms lives and promotes positive and lasting socials, economic, and spiritual change within a community; when it is based on mutual trust and fully shared accomplishment; and when it demonstrates responsible stewardship of all resources entrusted to us.

Our Strategic Pillars

  1. Spiritual Growth Ensuring effective Pastoral and lay ministry and effective discipleship.
  2. Education Educational development at all levels of ministry and in discipleship
  3. Governance Accountable, authentic, transformative and servant leadership for for the benefit of followers and communities.
  4. Social Activity Active engagement in social responsibility at the grassroots for ministry and revevence.
  5. Economy Improvement of the economic lives of the communities and individuals through economic training, empowerment and capacity building.
  6. Infrastructure Updrading of churches' Infrastructural facilities to match wuth the current standards and required by law.
  7. Technology Utilizing every relevant technological advancement while being in touch with state of the art technological programs and softwares to aid in administration, management, planning, assestment and data analysis.
